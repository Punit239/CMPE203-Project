Know Who We Are !!




Pinboard is a place, where you can tack anything you feel is worth bookmarking. From beauty to technology, it lets our users create their own Boards and pin anything and everything they come across over on Internet. Our users can create their own pins in the form of a blogspot and share it with the world so that others can pin it up on their boards.Pinboard unique integration with the social networking sites allows others to like and comment on our users pins and boards. Additionally, it recommends pins that might interest our users based on their own pinned up boards and it suggests users with similar interests.
